LAND (STATUTORY WAYLEAVES) ACCRA EAST MAMOBI UPGRADING PROGRAMME (EI 38).

 

WHEREAS the Provisional National Defence Council is of the opinion that it is in the public interest that a right of way over the land hereinafter described be created in respect of a highway.

NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred upon the Provisional National Defence Council by subsection (l) of section 1 of the Lands (Statutory Wayleaves) Act, 1963 (Act 186) this Instrument is made this 26th day of August, 1988.

The land specified in the Schedule hereto is hereby declared to be land subject to a right of way as a highway to ensure to the benefit of the public generally.

S C H E D U L E S

ALL the access road network and pieces or parcels of land referred to as K-VIPS situated at East Mamobi and adjoining the Nima-Mamobi Highway in the City of Accra in the Greater Accra Region of the Republic of Ghana comprising a total area of 4.08 acres the boundaries whereof as described in the schedule below—
