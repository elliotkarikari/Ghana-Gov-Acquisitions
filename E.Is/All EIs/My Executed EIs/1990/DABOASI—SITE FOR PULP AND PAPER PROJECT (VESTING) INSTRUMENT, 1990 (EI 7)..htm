DABOASI—SITE FOR PULP AND PAPER PROJECT (VESTING) INSTRUMENT, 1990 (EI 7).

 

WHEREAS under the provisions of subsection (1) of section 7 of the Administration of Lands Act, 1962 (Act 123) the Provisional National Defence Council in the public interest may declare any stool land to be vested in it in trust.

NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred on the Provisional National Defence Council by sub-section (1) of section 7 of the Administration of Lands Act, 1962 (Act 123) this Instrument is made this 27th day of February, 1990.

The land specified in the Schedule hereto is hereby declared to be vested in the Provisional National Defence Council in trust.

SCHEDULE

All those two pieces or parcels of land marked 'A' and 'B' and containing an approximate total area of 44, 325.27 acres, situate at Daboasi in the Wassa Fiase Mpohor District in the Western Region of the Republic of Ghana.

Firstly all that piece of land marked "A"—lying to the North-west of the Sekondi-Krobo motor road and bounded on or towards the North-east by an open space and measuring 1,716 feet more or less on or towards the South-east by an open space and measuring a total distance of 1,712 feet more or less on or towards the South-west by an open space and measuring a total distance of 2,826 feet more or less on or towards the North-west by an open space and measuring 716 feet more or less which piece or parcel of land is more particularly delineated on Plan No. LD.10197/1/73147 hereto attached and thereon shewn edged pink.

Secondly all that piece of land marked 'B'—lying to the West of Daboasi-Odumasi motor road and to the West of River Pra and bounded on the North by an open space and measuring 54,000 feet more or less on the East by an open space and measuring 29,500 feet more or less on the South-east by an open space and measuring a total distance of 39,800 feet more or less on or towards the South-west by an open space and measuring a total distance of 27,750 feet more or less on or towards the West by an open space and measuring a total distance of 60,500 feet more or less which piece or parcel of land is more particularly delineated on Plan No. LD.10197/2/73147 hereto attached and thereon shewn edged Pink.

By command of the Provisional National Defence Council.

RICHARD KWAME PEPRAH

P.N.D.C. Secretary for Lands and Natural Resources
