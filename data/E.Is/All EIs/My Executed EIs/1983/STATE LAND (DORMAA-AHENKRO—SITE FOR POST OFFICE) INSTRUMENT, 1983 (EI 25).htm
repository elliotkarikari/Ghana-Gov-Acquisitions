STATE LAND (DORMAA-AHENKRO—SITE FOR POST OFFICE) INSTRUMENT, 1983 (EI 25)

 

WHEREAS it appears to the Provisional National Defence Council that the land specified in the Schedule to this Instrument is land required in the public interest.

NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred on the Provisional National Defence Council by subsection (1) of section 1 of the State Lands Act, 1962 (Act 125) this Instrument is made this 18th day of August, 1983.

The land specified in the Schedule to this Instrument is hereby declared to be land required in the public interest.

SCHEDULE

All that piece of land containing an approximate area of 4.21 acres situate at Dormaa-Ahenkro in the Brong-Ahafo Central District in the Brong-Ahafo Region of the Republic of Ghana lying to the North of Agyeman Badu Middle School and to the South of the motor road from Ivory Coast to Berekum and bounded on the North-east by an open space and measuring on that side 380 feet more or less on the South-east partly by the site for Ghana Commercial Bank and partly by an open space and measuring on that side 530 feet more or less on the South-west by an unnamed road and measuring on that side 280 feet more or less and on the west by an unnamed road and measuring on that side a total distance of 530 feet more or less which piece of land is more particularly delineated on Plan No. LD.9686/67233 and thereon shewn edged pink.

By Command of the Provisional National Defence Council.

JOHN GODWIN AWOONOR RENNER

PNDC Secretary for Lands and Natural Resources
