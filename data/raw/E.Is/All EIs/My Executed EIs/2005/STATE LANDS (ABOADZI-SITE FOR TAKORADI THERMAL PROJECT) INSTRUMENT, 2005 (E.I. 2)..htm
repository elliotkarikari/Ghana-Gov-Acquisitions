STATE LANDS (ABOADZI-SITE FOR TAKORADI THERMAL PROJECT) INSTRUMENT, 2005 (E.I. 2).

  

WHEREAS it appears to the President that the Land described in the Schedule to this Instrument is land required in the public interest.

NOW THEREFORE, in exercise of the powers conferred on the President by sub-section 1 of section 1 of State Lands Act, 1962 (Act 125). This Instrument is made this 18th day of February, 2005.

The land specified in the schedule hereto is hereby declared to be land required in the public interest.

SCHEDULE

ALL THAT PIECE OF LAND known as Site for Volta River Authority (Takoradi Thermal Project) containing an approximate area of 421.18 acres (170.44 hectares) and situate at Aboadze in the Shama Ahanta East Metropolitan area in the Western Region of the Republic of Ghana bounded on the North by Open Space and measuring a total distance of 3097.2 feet more or less on the East by Open Space measuring a total distance of 5785.7 feet more or less on the South by the Gulf of Guinea measuring a total distance of 3107.9 feet more or less on the West by Open Space measuring a total distance of 6039.4 feet more or less as the same piece of land is more particularly delineated by the Plan No. LC 88/83766 attached hereto and thereon shown edged pink.

By Command of the President

PROF. DOMINIC FOBIH

Minister for Lands, Forestry and Mines

Date of Gazette Notification: 4th March, 2005.
